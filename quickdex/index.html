Create Your Charadex

This is just a tool that read outs your charadex sheet! If you don't want to set your own charadex page or charadex is decided to glitch out, this should work well enough!

This will not save any settings, so make sure to keep your link in a safe place.

If you haven't got a sheet yet, you can copy it here. Then just fill it out, make the link public to viewers, and add your ID in below!

      About

      After Eclipse took out DeviantArt, I made Charadex to help other species creators organize and keep easier track of their masterlist.

      And it's not limited to species! If you just sell adopts or what to make a little organizer for your characters - this might help. I use it to keep track of role play AU's and my own designs too!
